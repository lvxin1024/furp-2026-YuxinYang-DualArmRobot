## Meeting 01 - FURP Kickoff

- Date: 2026-06-08
- Organizer: Undergraduate Research Group, Faculty of Science and Engineering, UNNC
- Topic: Rules, workflow, and expectations for FURP

### Key points

1. FURP is a hands-on research program focused on applying classroom knowledge, exploring research interests, and producing real outputs.
2. Certificate eligibility requires meeting all three conditions:
- Attend more than 50% of program activities.
- Submit a poster PDF in the repository root named FURP_Showcase.pdf.
- Present at the Poster Showcase (in person preferred, stand-in acceptable if necessary).
3. For the Research Track, the minimum certification requirement is:
- Replicate a cited paper.
- Add at least 10% innovation beyond replication.
4. Weekly workflow requirements on GitHub:
- Update docs/00_weekly.md every week.
- Log key meeting takeaways in docs/meeting_notes/.
- Keep project materials and code in src/.
5. Leave or withdrawal must be sent by email. Chat or verbal notice is not counted as an official record.

### Action items for this project

1. Keep weekly updates consistent in docs/00_weekly.md.
2. Maintain meeting notes for each meeting in docs/meeting_notes/.
3. Confirm and lock one cited paper for replication with clear innovation goals.
4. Plan poster milestones early and reserve time for Showcase presentation.
5. Maintain attendance above 50% across the full cycle.

### Notes

- Core formula for certificate: Attend >50% + Submit poster + Present at Showcase.
- Recommended practice: update GitHub weekly to surface blockers early and keep progress visible.
